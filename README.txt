HOW TO PUBLISH
1. Replace https://your-domain.com in index.html, robots.txt, and sitemap.xml with your real site URL.
2. Replace sample broadcaster names with official confirmed broadcasters.
3. Update match data in script.js daily.
4. Upload all files to GitHub Pages, Netlify, or Vercel.
5. After publishing, add your site to Google Search Console and submit sitemap.xml.
6. Share match pages/updates on Instagram, WhatsApp, Telegram, and YouTube Shorts descriptions.
